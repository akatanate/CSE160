Earth 3000

What if WALL-E never found the plant, and thus, never left Earth? ...This world is set in the year 3000, where Earth remains uninhabitable and desserted, and WALL-E has become petrified via dirt and dust, no longer operational as the years flow by.

Movement:

W- Forward
S- Backward
D- Right
A- Left
E- Rotate Right
Q- Rotate Left
Up / down arrow
Camera rotates via moving mouse

Mission Brief:
Eve was forcibly shut down and hidden.
Find her so she can re-activate and save WALL-E!
